Desserts

Watalappan (Sri Lankan Coconut Custard)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • ½ cup jaggery (grated)
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 tbsp cashew nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs in a bowl.
  2. Add grated jaggery and mix until dissolved.
  3. Stir in coconut milk and cardamom powder.
  4. Pour into a greased bowl, cover with foil, and steam for 30–40 minutes until set.
  5. Garnish with cashews and serve chilled.

Chocolate Biscuit Pudding

Ingredients

  • 200g Marie biscuits
  • 1 cup strong black coffee
  • 200g butter
  • 200g icing sugar
  • 100g cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp vanilla essence

Steps

  1. Mix butter, icing sugar, cocoa, and vanilla into a smooth cream.
  2. Dip biscuits in coffee and layer in a dish.
  3. Spread chocolate cream over biscuit layer.
  4. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up.
  5. Refrigerate for 4–6 hours and serve chilled.

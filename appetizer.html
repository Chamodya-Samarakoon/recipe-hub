Coconut Roti With Lunumiris

Ingredients

Coconut Roti :

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup grated fresh coconut
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Water

Lunu Miris :

  • 1 small red onion (finely chopped)
  • 1–2 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • Maldive fish (optional)

Steps

Coconut Roti

  1. Mix flour, coconut, onion, chili, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add water gradually to form a soft dough.
  3. Divide into balls and flatten into discs.
  4. Cook on a hot skillet (no oil) until both sides are golden.

Lunu miris

  1. Mix onion, chili flakes, salt, and lime juice in a bowl.
  2. Add Maldive fish if desired.
  3. Serve with warm pol roti.

Vegetable Roti (Stuffed Roti Triangles)

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Water (as needed to knead)

Filling:

  • 1 large potato (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 carrot (grated)
  • ½ cup cabbage (shredded)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Coconut Oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, salt, and oil. Gradually add water to form a soft dough. Knead well and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. To prepare the filling, heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan and sauté onion and green chilies.
  3. Add grated carrot, cabbage, mashed potato, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5–7 minutes until well combined and soft. Let it cool.
  4. Divide the dough into equal portions. Roll each into a thin circle on a floured surface.
  5. Place 2–3 tablespoons of filling on one half of the circle. Fold the dough over the filling into a half-moon or triangle shape.
  6. Seal the edges by pressing gently with fingers or a fork.
  7. Heat a skillet and cook each roti with a little oil until golden brown on both sides.
  8. Serve hot with chutney or chili sauce.
